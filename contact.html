Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December-April). However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.

What's included in the charter price?

All charters include the yacht, professional crew, fuel, safety equipment, basic beverages, and standard amenities. Catering, special decorations, and additional services can be added based on your needs.

What happens if the weather is bad?

Safety is our top priority. If weather conditions are unsafe, we'll work with you to reschedule your charter or provide alternative options. We monitor weather closely and communicate any concerns in advance.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We work with experienced caterers who can accommodate virtually any dietary requirement, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other special needs. Just let us know when booking.

Do you provide transportation to the marina?

We can arrange transportation from your hotel or other locations in Puerto Vallarta for an additional fee. We'll provide detailed directions and meeting instructions for all charters.

What should guests bring?

We recommend bringing sunscreen, comfortable clothing, a light jacket for evening charters, and any personal items you might need. We provide towels, snorkeling gear, and other equipment.
